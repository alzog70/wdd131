Welcome to the World of the Cosmere

The Cosmere is a vast, interconnected universe that spans multiple planets, each with its own distinct magic systems, cultures, and characters. While each story takes place on a different world, they are linked by shared themes, ancient mysteries, and recurring elements that suggest a larger, underlying connection. As readers journey through the Cosmere, they uncover secrets about the nature of reality, the forces shaping the universe, and the relationships between the various worlds and their inhabitants.

Get to Know Brandon Sanderson

Brandon Sanderson is a prolific fantasy and science fiction author, best known for his work on the Cosmere universe, which connects many of his books through shared mythology and characters. Sanderson's storytelling is renowned for its intricate magic systems, compelling characters, and unpredictable plot twists. His works include "The Stormlight Archive," "Mistborn," and numerous novellas and standalone novels.

In addition to being a best-selling author, Sanderson is also a professor at Brigham Young University, where he teaches creative writing. His engaging lectures and behind-the-scenes insights into the world of fantasy writing have made him a beloved figure in the literary community.
