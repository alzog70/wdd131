Site Plan for "Cosmere Chronicles"

Site Name

Cosmere Chronicles - The site name reflects how this site will chronicle all of the books Brandon Sanderson has written and provide descriptions for them. cosmerechronicles.com.

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for the fans of Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere works. This will provide some brief information on the books and what orders you can read them in.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: Someone wonders what order to read Brandon Sanderson's books.
  • Scenario 2: Someone is wondering what other books he has in the Cosmere Univserse.
